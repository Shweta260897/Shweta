Welcome to my homepage

Hello and welcome! I'm Shweta Kumari, currently pursuing my PhD in Mathematics at the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi. My academic journey has been deeply rooted in mathematical modeling, numerical analysis, and the development of efficient computational methods. I am passionate about applying rigorous mathematics to solve complex differential equations that arise in real-world phenomena.

Research Interests

  • Time-fractional differential equations
  • Finite difference and nonstandard numerical schemes
  • Computational methods on metric graphs
  • Stability and consistency analysis of discrete approximations
  • Applications to anomalous diffusion and nonlocal dynamics
